ISO/IEC 17025 is the international standard that specifies the general requirements for the competence, impartiality, and consistent operation of testing and calibration laboratories. It is published jointly by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) and the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC).
A laboratory that meets ISO/IEC 17025 can be formally accredited — meaning an independent, authorised body has verified that the laboratory is technically competent and produces reliable results. In Kenya, this body is KENAS (Kenya Accreditation Service).
